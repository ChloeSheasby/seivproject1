Why should you be a computer science major?

"Well, it has great career opportunities. Computer scientists are in high demand, and computer science gives you the chance to learn for rest of your life. I really enjoy building computer science systems and seeing them used." - David North, Program Chair for Computer Science at Oklahoma Christian University

"You should be a computer science major because it is a very entertaining and rewarding major." - Pat Smith, Associate Professor of Science & Engineering at Oklahoma Christian University

"If you want to have any idea what is going on in the world 20 years from now, you need to know how to code." - Robert Nix, Assistant Professor Of Computer Science at Oklahoma Christian University

"It pays super well, and you get to work on fun problems. There are some amounts of creativity which some need in a fulfilling job, and it's not crazy hours. A computer science degree is one of the easiest ways to get into the industry." - Daniel Griffin, Computer Science Adjunct Faculty at Oklahoma Christian University

"It's the best major." - Don Leftwich, Associate Professor Of Mathematics and Computer Science at Oklahoma Christian University

"If you would like to be able to fight against cyber crime, it's the best way to do that. Learn computer science with cyber security." - Curtis Coleman, Director of Cybersecurity and Assistant Professor Of Computer Science at Oklahoma Christian University

"A computer science major is a great choice because you are learning to be in the driver's seat of technology that can make a huge impact on your future and the world." - Andy Harbert, Instructor at Francis Tuttle Computer Science Academy
